Our Work

Discover how our Smart Water Quality Management System enhances Pangasius farming through automation and innovation.

Recirculating Aquaculture System (RAS)

Our system integrates a Recirculating Aquaculture System that continuously filters and reuses water in the Cream Dory tanks. This setup helps maintain clean and stable water conditions, reduces waste, and supports sustainable fish farming while conserving resources.

Smart Monitoring System

The Smart Monitoring System uses Arduino and ESP32 microcontrollers with sensors to track pH, temperature, salinity, dissolved oxygen, ammonia, and water level in real time. Data are displayed on a web dashboard and LCD screen, allowing farmers to easily monitor and manage tank conditions.
