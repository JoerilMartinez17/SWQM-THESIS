Our Hypothalamus Technology

Innovative monitoring systems that revolutionize pangasius aquaculture

The Science Behind Our Success

Our Smart Water Quality Management System (SWQM) tracks key biological indicators in pangasius to optimize feeding, water conditions, and growth parameters.

By analyzing hypothalamus responses, we can detect stress, hunger, and other conditions before physical symptoms appear, allowing for immediate adjustments to farming conditions.

This technology has increased our yields by 35% while reducing feed costs and improving fish health metrics.

Key Features

Real-Time Monitoring

Continuous tracking of hypothalamus activity through non-invasive sensors provides instant data on fish wellbeing.

  • Tracks stress levels and feeding responses
  • Monitors environmental adaptation
  • Detects early signs of health issues

Data-Driven Decisions

Our AI-powered analytics system processes hypothalamus data to recommend optimal farming conditions.

  • Automated feeding schedule adjustments
  • Water quality parameter optimization
  • Growth prediction modeling

Healthier Fish

Reduced disease incidence and improved fish welfare metrics.
